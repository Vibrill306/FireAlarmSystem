SITC Services

S : Supply

We provide a wide range of high-quality fire alarm equipment and components, ensuring that our clients have access to the latest and most reliable technology available in the market.

I : Installation

Our team of skilled technicians and engineers will professionally install the fire alarm system, ensuring that it is seamlessly integrated into the premises and meets all applicable codes and regulations.

T : Testing

We conduct thorough testing of the fire alarm system to ensure its proper functionality. This includes testing the sensors, control panels, notification devices, and other components to verify that they are working optimally.

C : Commissioning

Our commissioning services involve the final verification and certification of the fire alarm system. We ensure that all components are properly calibrated and synchronized, and conduct a comprehensive inspection to guarantee that the system meets the required standards and specifications.
